
# About
I am an experienced EDA and VLSI engineer with a strong background in application engineering, software quality assurance, and digital/mixed-signal design. My work spans the entire semiconductor design and verification flow — from circuit design and validation to automation and customer application support.

As an Application Engineer at Silvaco Inc., I support and optimize advanced EDA tools, focusing on quality assurance, testing, validation, automation, and workflow optimization. I design efficient processes and automated systems that enhance reliability and streamline product development across platforms.

I actively apply data analysis and automation in my daily work, using Python, Bash, and related tools to automate data generation, analysis, and visualization. I also develop custom applications and dashboards that present results clearly, supporting effective testing, reporting, and decision-making.

Previously, at Synopsys Armenia, I started as a VLSI Design Engineer, building test chips for our IPs, and later transitioned to EDA software QA, growing from a mid-level engineer to Senior and Team Lead, improving quality and process efficiency across multiple projects.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring embedded systems, IoT, audio and RF design, and RC model engineering, building everything from home servers and amplifiers to quadcopters and fixed-wing aircraft.
